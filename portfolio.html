Fixed Deposit (FD) Calculator - SEM 1 (04/03/2023-03/05/2023)

This is a Fixed Deposit Calculator that I created using Power Apps during SEM 1. I used "set" and "clear collect" on the "calculate" button, and the calculation result will appear in the "result" field. I also used "round" in the "calculate" button, so the result will have only two decimal places. The icons are used to clear the values of Deposit Amount, Annual Interest Rate, Time Period, and the result. There is also a reminder that appears at the bottom. If the result does not show a number, it will display "Please Enter Your Deposit Amount, Annual Interest, and Time Period". If a number appears, it will show "Your Fixed Deposit Result is xx".
